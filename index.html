Welcome to Abdi Ahmed Primary School

Abdi Ahmed Primary School is a warm, welcoming, and inclusive learning community that provides high-quality education to children aged 5 to 11 years. Our school is located in a beautiful and peaceful setting, and our dedicated staff work hard to create a safe, caring, and stimulating environment where every child can flourish and achieve their full potential. At Abdi Ahmed Primary School, we believe that every child is unique and has their own talents, interests, and learning needs. Therefore, we provide a broad and balanced curriculum that is designed to meet the needs of all children, including those with special educational needs and disabilities. Our curriculum is enriched with a wide range of extra-curricular activities, including sports, music, drama, and art, to help children develop their skills, interests, and confidence. Our staff are committed to providing the highest standards of teaching and learning. They are experienced, highly qualified, and dedicated to helping children achieve their best. We believe that working in partnership with parents and carers is essential to ensuring that every child reaches their full potential. We encourage parents and carers to be involved in their child's learning journey and to share their thoughts, ideas, and concerns with us. We hope you find our website informative and helpful. If you have any questions or would like to visit our school, please do not hesitate to contact us. Thank you for considering Abdi Ahmed Primary School for your child's education.
